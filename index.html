Frequently asked questions

Allowing learners to find the information they need independently

What degrees are offered at the RAK Campus?

We offer globally recognized UK undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in Accounting & Finance, Computing Science, and Software Engineering, identical to those awarded at our UK home campus.

Is the degree recognized internationally?

Yes, your degree is awarded by the University of Stirling, UK, which is QS 5-Star rated and globally accredited. It holds the same value and recognition as a degree earned in the UK.

Are scholarships available for new students?

Absolutely! We offer Academic Merit Scholarships of up to 50% for high-achieving students. Contact our admissions team to check your eligibility.

Can I transfer to the UK campus?

Yes, students have the opportunity to transfer to the UK home campus after completing their first or second year in RAK, subject to academic performance.
